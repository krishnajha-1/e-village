Traditional Practices

Kalap has a rich cultural heritage with local customs, festivals, and folk traditions. The villagers often celebrate traditional festivals like Makar Sankranti and Bhitauli, which involve vibrant local dances and music.

Handicrafts

The village is home to artisans skilled in traditional crafts such as weaving and woodwork, reflecting the local culture and history.

Cuisine

The local diet includes staple foods like rice, lentils, and seasonal vegetables, with unique dishes that highlight the region's agricultural produce.
